The Point of No Return

A game developed for rasing the awareness of Climate Change

       In the near future, the Earth has been destroyed by the forces of Climate Change. Our hero, Bee Knight, must go back in time and revert the elements of global warming so she can defeat the ultimate evil: Captain Climate Change.

Goal

Create a game that educates younger audiences about the real risks of climate change.

Mechanics

Point of No Return(PONT) is a 2-D platformer that uses theme characters and interwoven facts to inform the player of the importance of climate change awareness
